# Canon Ledger

This document is the "Single Source of Truth" for world facts, motifs, and timeline anchors in Luminous Deep.

## 1. Recurring Objects
*These objects appear across multiple scenes/domains and must maintain continuity.*
- **The Brass Lantern:** A portable light source often found on workbenches or in hallways.
- **The Leather Journal:** Eleanor's primary diary; found on her desk in the Study.
- **The Seagrove Map:** A maritime chart of the bay; found in the Boathouse.

## 2. Timeline Anchors
- **Foundation Day:** The date the lighthouse was decommissioned.
- **The Arrival:** The moment the user/visitor first steps onto the dock.

## 3. Forbidden Vibes
*Any content pack violating these will be rejected during Canon Check.*
- агрессивный (Aggressive) advertising or marketing speak.
- Voyeuristic or intrusive camera angles.
- Horror, gore, or jump-scares.
- Explicit "AI-generated" artifacts (hallucinations, multi-limbed figures).
